overview

The point of this website is to demonstrate the ability to utilize a pre-existing HTML website template and modify it to fit the needs of the client. The client is Silver Oak Landscaping. It matches the branding of the company, as well as it's original userflow.

challenge

The challenge of this concept was replicating the client's original branding across an HTML environment built by someone else. Often, web designers face the challenge of working through code that was not created by them. This concept was no different. I was in a unique position between dictated content and predefined design. This challenge gave me the opportunity to exercise critical thinking and problem solving skills to craft a creative solution.

approach

I began with researching other landscaping companies in the area to get a feel for this company's clientele. I then read the documentation of the template chosen to understand its layout and resolve how the content given to me would fit while still fulfilling the companies objectives.

conclusion

This demonstrated my skills to work in an environment that required modification, restylization, and repairment of the original material and design. This prepared me for working with design and development of another designer or developer. The final result of this concept was a website that promotes Silver Oak Landscaping's branding, as well as enables future maintenance and modification for new content.
